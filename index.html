WHY COME TO THIS GYM?

Have you ever thought of gymming but cannot find an easily accessible and convenient gym to go to? Fret not, everyone can now go to this gym to work out. In order to have work-life balance, we should take breaks in between our studying and work! Sitting for long periods of time to study and work is detrimental to your health and fitness levels. So, let's get fit together to gain a healthy lifestyle!!

Why Gym?

Benefits of working out:

  • It makes you happier
  • It can help with weight loss
  • It is good for your muscle and bones
  • It can increase your energy levels
  • It can reduce your risk of chronic disease
  • It is good for your physical and mental health

PROGRAMMES

Be spoilt for choice! Workout Gym offers a comprehensive range of studio programmes for all fitness levels. There are functional fitness training programmes and virtual fitness programmes too!

Workout Gym Functional Fitness

Functional fitness exercises are designed to strengthen muscles, improve stability, and prevent injuries. It enhances the way you accomplish daily activities such as carrying grocery bags, walking up stairs or playing sports. Suitable for all ages, it can be done anywhere, at any time! Call Workout Gym centers today to enquire on slots availability, learn more about functional fitness from our professional fitness instructors or sign up for the programmes available!

Workout Gym Virtual Fitness

Workout Gym fitness programmes are now online! Introducing virtual fitness classes where you can work out in the comfort and privacy of your own home. All you need is a Zoom account and a good Wi-Fi connection, and you can enjoy the fun and energy of our Workout Gym programmes wherever you are! Sign up now and start your online workouts with your favourite instructors!
